About us

AngelEyeAI is built upon the idea that machine learning can be applied to help provide solutions for healthcare solutions. AngelEyeAI helps medical school students by providing image classification for Covid-19 and Pneumonia x-ray scans. It also provides an image based search page for Covid-19 and Pneumonia that produces image classification, images related to the classification, resourceful urls, and a quick summary made to create learning efficient. We also strive to apply this technology in education, research, ad at-home care with our upload and image search functionality.
